Our Services

Library Services

Our library offers a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support your academic and research needs. Access online databases, request interlibrary loans, and enjoy a quiet study environment with our comprehensive library services.

IT Support

We provide technical support for students and staff, addressing issues related to software, hardware, and network connectivity. Our IT support team is available to help with troubleshooting, installations, and general technical assistance.

Career Counseling

Our career counseling services offer guidance on career exploration, internships, and job placements. Meet with career advisors to develop your resume, prepare for interviews, and plan your career path effectively.

How to Access Our Services

Most of our services can be accessed online through our dedicated portal. For in-person assistance, visit the respective departments during working hours. Detailed instructions and support contacts are available on our portal for easy access.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the library's digital resources?

You can access digital resources through the library portal using your institution login credentials. Visit the library website for more information on how to log in and utilize available resources.

Who do I contact for IT support?

For IT support, email us at itsupport@institution.com or visit the IT department during office hours for in-person assistance. Our support team is available to help with any technical issues you may encounter.
